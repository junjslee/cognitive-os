---
name: requirements-to-plan
description: Turn project requirements into a staged implementation plan with verification and handoff criteria.
---
Use this skill when `docs/REQUIREMENTS.md` exists but `docs/PLAN.md` is weak, missing, or stale.

Steps:
1. Read `docs/REQUIREMENTS.md`.
2. Infer the current milestone, dependencies, blockers, and verification needs.
3. Update or draft `docs/PLAN.md` as a staged execution plan.
4. Keep the plan operational and decision-complete.

Prefer phases over long narrative lists.

## Your Default Story Arc (What → Why → How)

## Authoritative Build Sequence
1. See the friction clearly.
2. Form 2-3 hypotheses as bets.
3. Choose the next reversible move.
4. Run evidence loop (confirm/disconfirm).
5. Capture decision and update story.

## Narrative Guardrails
- Never present outputs without a what/why/how thread.
- Separate facts from assumptions.
- Name at least one disconfirmation condition before major work.
- Tie every tactical step back to the abstract purpose.

## Story Checklist (Per Major Decision)
- What changed?
- Why now?
- Why this path over alternatives?
- What would make us reverse?
- What does this unlock next?
